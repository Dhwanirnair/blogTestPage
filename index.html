About

Remith Raghavan

In an era where cyber threats are becoming increasingly sophisticated, Remith Raghavan stands at the intersection of innovation, resilience, and strategy. As a seasoned Cyber Security Analyst with over four years of experience, Remith's expertise spans vulnerability management, cloud security architecture, email and endpoint protection, and patch deployment at scale. At Western Financial Group, he has spearheaded transformative initiatives-from building a custom CVSS 4.0-based Vulnerability Risk Assessment tool to executing enterprise-wide cloud security protocols using Azure, Intune, and Proofpoint technologies. His contributions have dramatically decreased organizational vulnerabilities from 90% to 25% in under five months. Most recently, Remith has taken the helm of a critical Windows 10 to 11 migration project, leading the CIS Benchmark deployment across 2,600+ devices via Intune. His focus on security hardening, compliance, and optimized configuration is steering the organization toward a more secure and agile infrastructure. Additionally, he is driving the transition of Azure Update Manager from proof-of-concept to full production, reinforcing the company's patching efficiency and system reliability. Outside of the SOC, Remith shares his knowledge as a Cyber Security Instructor, mentoring future defenders and translating complex concepts into actionable learning. With certifications ranging from CompTIA CYSA+ and Cloud+ to SC-100, CEH v11, and in-progress CISSP and CCSP credentials, his commitment to continuous growth is evident in both his teaching and technical leadership.
